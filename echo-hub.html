SUMMARY

Echo Hub is a brand new Echo Family Device designed for the smart home power user. The wall-mounted device gives customers easy access and control to all of their smart home devices. The innovative navigation model allows customers to control their entire home regardless of where the device is placed. With a focus on the smart home, this device melds all of customers favorite Alexa-enabled features into one space.

MY WORK

I worked on this system’s Core UX, exploring how devices can leverage customers' proximity to their devices to create value-driven and delightful experiences. As well, I worked on the scalability of the Active Media system to Echo Hub. I created refreshed and thoughtful designs to provide customers with seamless media streaming experiences. In addition, I designed the routines panel and conducted research on how to best include routines on Echo Hub.
