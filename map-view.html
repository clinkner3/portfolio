CONTRIBUTIONS

Interaction Design, Design Sprints, User Research

SUMMARY

Map View provides a simplified way for customers to interact with all of their devices in one glanceable location. Giving customers the ability to have at-a-glance access to their smart home creates new opportunities for Alexa smart home and eases unnecessary stress on customers, such as remembering the names for endless smart lights.

MY WORK

I contributed to various aspects of Map View, including the first time user enrollment and floor plan set up. I worked within the realm of AR design and 2D to 3D models, as well as defined guidelines for scalability of Map View across devices.
